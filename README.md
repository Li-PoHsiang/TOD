## Data Availability

This repository provides part of the data used in our article. The data currently included are mainly the results obtained after restricting the sample to Chinese enterprises. For any additional data requirements, please contact us by email.

If you use this dataset, please cite Xiang et al. (2025). In addition, the data retrieval procedure in this article was developed based on the work of Ran and Tian (2024).

### References

[2] Ran, C., & Tian, W. (2024). Identification of potential emerging technologies by fusing SVM-LDA and weighted similarity: Taking the field of artificial intelligence as an example. *Journal of the China Society for Scientific and Technical Information, 43*(5), 563–574.
